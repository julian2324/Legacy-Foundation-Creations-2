Julian "The Reverb" Crenshaw

Julian, also known as The Reverb, has been dancing for over 24 years and teaching for over 11 years. He has studied dance and instruction in Anchorage, Alaska and has been traveling around the world teaching workshops, performing and battling at World of Dance events, and learning from famous dancers who came from different regions of the world. Julian has won multiple awards and achivements from different battels and competitions. He uses his gift not only to teach movement but to also minister the gospel through dance. Julian instructs on how to use dance as an outlet for any type of emotion and to express by freestyling movements that you have learned. Julian moved out to Austin Texas in 2017 to teach, perform and battle and has also met his wife in Austin. Together they pushed each other in their dancing and broke down barriers holding them back. In September of 2018 they started teaching at IDEA Public Schools where they created a scholarship program that gave students the opportunity to push beyond their limits in dance and life. Julian is the curret Professor of Hip Hop Dance and Cultures at Oklahoma State University where he teaches history, culture and foundation in the hip hop genre. Julian believes that dance brings people together even in a divided world and he will continue to bring light and truth to the dance floor weather in the studio or on the streets.
